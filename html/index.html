Top 3 Renaissance Paintings

I'm very passionate on art, so here is my top 3 Reinassance paintings. (One of my favorite movements)

  1. Mona Lisa by Leonardo da Vinci

    Painted between 1503 and 1506, the Mona Lisa is one of the most famous paintings in the world. It's celebrated for its exquisite detail and enigmatic smile.

  2. The Last Supper by Leonardo da Vinci

    This iconic mural, painted between 1495 and 1498, depicts the moment Jesus announces that one of his disciples will betray him. It's known for its emotional depth and perspective.

  3. The School of Athens by Raphael

    Painted between 1509 and 1511, this fresco is a masterpiece of Renaissance art. It portrays famous philosophers and scholars from different eras and is celebrated for its intellectual symbolism.
